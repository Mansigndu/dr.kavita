Why prevention matters

Kidney disease often develops silently without symptoms. By the time symptoms appear, significant damage may already be done.

Preventive care helps detect risks early and protects kidney function for the long term.

What we focus on

  • ✔ Early kidney screening
  • ✔ Blood pressure monitoring
  • ✔ Diabetes control
  • ✔ Lifestyle counseling
  • ✔ Routine kidney tests

Who should get tested?

• Diabetes patients
• High blood pressure patients
• Family history of kidney disease
• Elderly individuals
• Obesity or lifestyle risk
• Frequent urinary issues

Prevention is better than cure

Regular checkups can help protect your kidneys and avoid future complications.
