Dr. Kavita Vishwakarma is a Consultant Nephrologist and Renal Transplant Physician with over 15 years of clinical experience in kidney disease, hypertension, dialysis and kidney transplantation.

She completed her DM in Nephrology from the prestigious Sanjay Gandhi Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences (SGPGI), Lucknow and has trained at leading institutions including Medanta – The Medicity, Gurgaon and Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital, Mumbai.
