Warning Signs

Symptoms that should not be ignored

Swelling of feet, ankles or face
Persistent high blood pressure
Changes in urination patterns
Fatigue, weakness or loss of appetite
Expert guidance

When should you consult a nephrologist?

Early consultation can help prevent kidney damage and improve long-term health outcomes.

  • ✓ Uncontrolled blood pressure
  • ✓ Diabetes with kidney concerns
  • ✓ Persistent protein in urine
  • ✓ Recurrent urinary tract infections
  • ✓ Reduced kidney function tests
  • ✓ Kidney stone complications
