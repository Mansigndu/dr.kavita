Diabetes & kidney disease

Diabetes is one of the leading causes of kidney damage. Early detection and strict control can prevent long-term kidney failure.

How diabetes affects kidneys

High blood sugar damages small blood vessels in the kidneys. Over time, this reduces kidney function and may lead to chronic kidney disease.

Most patients do not feel symptoms in early stages — making regular screening very important.

Our treatment approach

✔ Early kidney screening in diabetic patients

✔ Urine protein & creatinine monitoring

✔ Blood sugar optimization

✔ Kidney protection medications (if required)
