Specialized Kidney Care

Glomerular Diseases

Diseases affecting kidney filters (glomeruli) that require specialized diagnosis and long-term treatment.

Book Consultation

Conditions We Treat

Nephrotic syndrome, IgA nephropathy, lupus nephritis, proteinuria and autoimmune kidney diseases.

Symptoms

Foamy urine, swelling, fatigue, high BP, abnormal urine tests.

Treatment Approach

✔ Kidney biopsy (if needed)

✔ Immunosuppressive therapy

✔ Protein control

✔ Long-term monitoring
