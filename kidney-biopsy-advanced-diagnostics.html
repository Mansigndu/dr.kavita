About Kidney Biopsy

Kidney biopsy is a medical procedure where a small tissue sample is taken from the kidney for examination under a microscope. It helps diagnose various kidney diseases and conditions that cannot be identified through blood or urine tests alone.

Our advanced diagnostic capabilities ensure accurate diagnosis for personalized treatment strategies.

Our diagnostic services include

  • ✔ Ultrasound-guided kidney biopsy
  • ✔ Urine protein and blood analysis
  • ✔ Immune-related kidney disease diagnosis
  • ✔ Transplant kidney biopsy evaluation
  • ✔ Comprehensive pathology reporting
