Acute Kidney Injury

A sudden decline in kidney function that requires immediate diagnosis and treatment.

Call Now

Common Causes

Infections, severe dehydration, medications, or low blood pressure can suddenly affect kidney function.

Warning Signs

Reduced urine output, swelling, fatigue, nausea, confusion.

Treatment Plan

✔ Immediate lab testing

✔ Fluid & electrolyte correction

✔ Medication review

✔ Hospital/ICU care if needed

✔ Kidney recovery monitoring
