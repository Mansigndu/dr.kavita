Professional Profile

Dedicated To Preserving Kidney Health Through Early Intervention

Dr. Kavita Vishwakarma is a Nephrologist and Renal Transplant Physician and the founder of Kaizen Kidney & BP Clinic, Mumbai.

With over 15 years of clinical experience, she is dedicated to the prevention, early detection and comprehensive management of kidney diseases across all stages.

Her approach combines evidence-based medicine, compassionate consultations and long-term patient-centered care.
