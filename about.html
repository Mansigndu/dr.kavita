Professional Profile

Expert kidney care with a compassionate approach

Dr. Kavita Vishwakarma is a Consultant Nephrologist and Renal Transplant Physician with over 15 years of clinical experience in kidney disease, hypertension, dialysis and kidney transplantation.

She completed her DM in Nephrology from the prestigious Sanjay Gandhi Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences (SGPGI), Lucknow, one of India's leading centres for nephrology and renal transplantation.

She has trained and worked at renowned institutions including Medanta – The Medicity, Gurgaon, and Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital, Mumbai.

Her approach combines evidence-based medicine, compassionate consultations and long-term patient-centred care, helping patients preserve kidney function and improve quality of life.

Dr. Kavita is passionate about preventive medicine and patient education. Through public speaking, writing and digital health education, she works to increase awareness about kidney disease, diabetes and hypertension.
