What is Dialysis?

Dialysis is a treatment that performs the function of kidneys when they are no longer able to filter waste and excess fluids from the body.

It is essential for patients with advanced kidney failure to maintain healthy body function.

Our Support Includes

  • ✔ Diet & fluid management
  • ✔ Long-term monitoring

When is Dialysis Needed?

• Severe kidney failure
• High creatinine levels
• Fluid overload
• Severe fatigue & weakness
• Loss of kidney function
• Electrolyte imbalance
