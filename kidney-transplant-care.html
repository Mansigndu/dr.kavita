About Kidney Transplant

Kidney transplant is a surgical procedure where a healthy kidney is placed in a patient whose kidneys have failed. It is considered the best long-term treatment for end-stage kidney disease.

Proper evaluation and follow-up care are essential for transplant success.

Our Care Includes

  • ✔ Pre-transplant evaluation
  • ✔ Donor matching guidance
  • ✔ Post-transplant monitoring
  • ✔ Immunosuppression management

Benefits of Kidney Transplant

• Better quality of life
• Freedom from dialysis
• Improved energy levels
• Long-term survival benefit
• Better diet flexibility
• Normal lifestyle possible
