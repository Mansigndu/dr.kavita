Why Blood Pressure Matters

Uncontrolled blood pressure is one of the leading causes of kidney damage. It can silently affect your kidneys, heart, and blood vessels over time.

Early detection and proper management are essential to prevent complications.

Signs of High Blood Pressure

• Headache
• Dizziness
• Chest discomfort
• Blurred vision
• Fatigue
• Nosebleeds (rare)
